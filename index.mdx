## What is Yenhyia?

Yenhyia is a comprehensive **Event OS** designed to empower event organizers, streamline sponsor interactions, and enable resellers to grow their networks. Our diverse platform focuses on helping attendees network better while providing robust tools for event management.

The platform relies on three core facets:
