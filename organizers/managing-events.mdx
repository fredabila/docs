Once you have created an event, the **Event Dashboard** becomes your command center. This central hub provides access to a suite of tools designed to help you manage every aspect of your event, from ticketing to attendee engagement.

## Accessing the Dashboard

1.  Navigate to the **My Events** tab in your main organizer interface.
2.  Locate the event you wish to manage from your list of created events.
3.  Click the **Manage** button on the event card.

## Dashboard Overview

Upon entering the dashboard, you will be presented with a high-level view of your event's performance and a navigation menu containing specialized toolsets.

### Essential Metrics
The main view displays critical real-time data:
- **Total Capacity**: Total signups and imports vs your limit.
- **Actual Check-ins**: Live count of verified attendees.
- **Platform Users**: Number of attendees with active Yenhyia accounts.

### Quick Actions
- **Get QR Code**: Access the scanner code for staff check-ins.
- **View Guestlist**: Quickly manage your attendee list.
- **Open Full Analytics**: Dive deeper into your event data.
