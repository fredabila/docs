Yenhyia integrates with powerful third-party platforms to help you manage data, communications, and marketing seamlessly.

## Accessing Integrations

To manage your integrations, navigate to the **Organizer Dashboard**. In the header navigation, you will find a series of tabs. Scroll or look towards the end of the list:

`My Events` > `Analytics` > `Marketing` > `Reports` > `Billing` > `API Keys` > **`Integrations`** > `Sponsors` > `Audience`

Click on the **Integrations** tab to view available connections.

## Available Integrations

Setting up integrations is designed to be simple. Click the integration card to open its settings page.

### Eventbrite
Sync your events and attendees directly from Eventbrite into Yenhyia.

**Setup Instructions:**
1.  Click the **Eventbrite** card.
2.  Click the **Connect** button.
3.  An authentication window will open. Log in to your Eventbrite account and authorize the connection.
4.  Once connected, you can use the sync tool to import event data and attendee lists.

### MNotify
Manage and send SMS notifications to your attendees using MNotify.

**Setup Instructions:**
1.  Click the **MNotify** card.
2.  You will be prompted to enter your **API Key**.
3.  To find your key, log in to your [MNotify Dashboard](https://mnotify.com).
4.  Go to **Developer** > **API Keys**.
5.  Copy the key and paste it into Yenhyia to complete the setup.

### Mailchimp
Handle your mailing lists and email marketing campaigns by connecting your Mailchimp account.

**Setup Instructions:**
1.  Click the **Mailchimp** card.
2.  Click the **Connect** button.
3.  An authentication window will open. Log in to your Mailchimp account and authorize access.
4.  Your audience lists will now be accessible for campaign management within Yenhyia.
