Welcome to Yenhyia! As an organizer, you are the backbone of our community. Follow these steps to set up your account and start listing events.

## Creating Your Account

1.  **Visit the Organizer Portal**
    Navigate to [https://yenhyia.com/for-organizers](https://yenhyia.com/for-organizers).

2.  **Start the Process**
    Click the **List Your Event** CTA button located in the hero section of the page.

3.  **Sign Up**
    You will be redirected to a signup screen. Complete the registration form to create your user account.

4.  **Build Your Profile**
    Once signed up, follow the on-screen prompts to complete your event organizer profile. This setup ensures you have access to the dashboard and all organizer features.
