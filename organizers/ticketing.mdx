</Note>

The **Ticketing** module handles all aspects of paid and free access to your event, including price tiers and door management.

## enabling Ticketing
If you haven't set up tickets yet, you will see a landing page. Click **Enable Ticketing** to activate the sales dashboard.

## Tier Management

Create different levels of access for your attendees (e.g., VIP, Regular, Student).

### Creating a New Tier
1.  Click **+ New Tier** in the top right.
2.  **Tier Identity**: Name your ticket (e.g., "VIP Early Access").
3.  **Price & Quantity**: Set the cost per ticket (GHS) and the total number available.
4.  **Detailed Access**: Describe what this ticket includes (e.g., "Backstage access, 2 free drinks").
5.  **Attachment Form**: Select a custom form if you need to collect specific info (e.g., dietary preferences) during checkout. Default is "General Checkout (No Form)".
6.  **Automated Promotions**: Schedule price changes.
    - **Promo Name**: e.g., "Early Bird".
    - **Sale Price**: The discounted amount.
    - **Dates**: Set the start and end time for the sale. The price will automatically revert to standard after the end date.
7.  **Status**: Ensure the checkbox **Mark as Active & Available** is checked.
8.  Click **Finalize Tier Config**.

## Access Control & Staff

Manage the authorized team members who can scan tickets and check in attendees at the door.

### Scanner Tools
- **Copy Scanner Link**: Generates a direct URL for the scanning interface. Share this with your temporary staff so they can start scanning immediately without logging into the full dashboard.
- **Launch Check-in WebApp**: Opens the scanner directly in your current browser.

### Managing Staff
To give a team member permanent access to the Scanner App:
1.  **Requirement**: The staff member must have a registered Yenhyia account.
2.  **Add Staff**: Enter their email address (e.g., `staff@example.com`) in the "Add New Staff Member" box.
3.  Click **+ Add**.
4.  They will now appear in the **Authorized Team** list and can log in to the scanner tools.

## Operations: Manual Issue
You can manually send a ticket to a guest without charging them (e.g., for sponsors or VIPs).
1.  Click **Issue Ticket**.
2.  **Recipient Details**: Enter Name and Email.
    > [!IMPORTANT]
    > **Account Required**: The recipient SHOULD sign up for a Yenhyia account with this email address to access their digital ticket wallet in the app.
3.  **Ticket Tier**: Select which pass they receive.
4.  **Notes**: Add an internal reference (optional).
5.  Click **Issue & Check-in**.

## Financials

Track your earnings and payouts.
1.  Click the **Financials** tab.
2.  **Overview**: View **Total Proceeds** (gross sales) and **Total Fees** (Platform + Payment Provider).
3.  **Withdrawals**:
    - Check your **Withdrawable** balance (green).
    - Click **Request Withdrawal** to transfer funds to your linked bank account or mobile money wallet.
    - View past transactions in the **Withdrawal History** table.
