---
title: "Listing Your Event"
description: "A step-by-step guide to publishing your first event on Yenhyia."
---

Once your organizer profile is set up, you can start listing events immediately.

## Step 1: Initiate Event Creation

From your **Organizer Dashboard**, look for the **green "Create Your Event" button** in the header navigation. Click it to begin.

## Step 2: Select Your Plan

A modal will appear asking you to choose a plan for this event.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Free Plan" icon="ticket">
    Perfect for free community meetups and small gatherings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pro Plan" icon="crown" color="#ea5a0c">
    Advanced features including custom artwork, analytics, and more.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Review the benefits listed on each card, select your preferred option, and click the **"Continue to details"** button.

## Step 3: Event Details

Fill out the form with your event information. Some fields are optional, while others are required to ensure your attendees have all the info they need.

### Essential Information

<ParamField body="Event Name" type="string" required>
  The official title of your experience.
  
  > [!TIP]
  > **Naming Best Practices:**
  > - **Do:** Include the year/date (e.g., "Tech Summit 2024").
  > - **Do:** Add location/venue if specific (e.g., "AI Meetup - San Francisco").
  > - **Do:** Use edition numbers for recurring events (e.g., "Startup Night Vol. 3").
  > - **Don't:** Us generic names like "Networking Event".
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="Event Image / Artwork" type="image" required>
  Upload a high-quality cover image for your event page.
  
  > [!NOTE]
  > **Pro Plan Only:** Custom image uploads are locked on the Free plan.
</ParamField>

### Date & Time

<ParamField body="Date" type="date" required>
  Format: `mm/dd/yyyy`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="Time" type="time" required>
  Set the start time for your event.
</ParamField>

### Location & Context

<ParamField body="Location" type="string" required>
  Search and select the venue on Google Maps to ensure accurate directions for attendees.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="Category" type="select" required>
  Choose the primary category (e.g., **Technology**).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="Tags (Interests)" type="array">
  Add relevant tags to help users find your event (e.g., `AI`, `Startup`).
</ParamField>

### Additional Details

<ParamField body="Description" type="text" required>
  Provide a detailed description of what attendees can expect.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="External URL / Website" type="url">
  Link to your event's external website or registration page (e.g., `https://your-event-website.com`).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="Capacity" type="number">
  Set the maximum number of attendees (e.g., **Max 100**).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="Ticket Price" type="money">
  Set the cost per ticket if applicable.
</ParamField>
