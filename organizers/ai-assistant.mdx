Yenhyia features a powerful integrated AI assistant designed to act as your co-organizer. Always accessible via the floating button in the bottom right corner of your screen, the assistant can help streamline complex tasks.

## Capabilities

The AI Assistant is trained to handle five key areas of event management:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Architect Events" icon="calendar-pen">
    Need help conceptualizing an event? The AI can help brainstorm ideas and even auto-populate event creation forms for you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Marketing Strategy" icon="bullhorn">
    Generate professional email campaigns, social media posts, and marketing copy tailored to your event's audience.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Personalization Expert" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Get advice on how to customize your onboarding flow and guest experience to increase engagement.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audience Analysis" icon="users-viewfinder">
    Ask questions about your attendee base to uncover demographics and interests without digging through spreadsheets.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Platform Guide" icon="book-open">
    Stuck on a feature? The assistant can guide you through venue building, ticketing setups, and more.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Use

1.  Click the **AI icon** in the bottom right corner of any page.
2.  Select one of the suggested prompts or type your own question.
3.  The assistant will perform the task or guide you to the right solution instantly.
