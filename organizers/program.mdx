<Note>
**Access Path**: **Event Dashboard** > **Program**
</Note>

The **Program** section is where you build the core experience of your event. You can schedule sessions, assign speakers, and manage live interactions like polls and Q&A. This section is divided into **Agenda** and **Speakers**.

## Agenda

Your agenda is the roadmap for your attendees.

### Adding a Session
1.  Click the **+ (Plus)** button in the **Sessions** sidebar.
2.  **Session Title**: Enter the name of the talk, workshop, or activity (e.g., "Opening Keynote: The Future of Tech").
3.  **Day**: Assign the session to a specific day of your event (e.g., "Day 1").
4.  **Track**: If your event has concurrent streams, specify the track (e.g., "Technical", "Business").
5.  **Speakers**: Select the speakers managing this session (you must add them in the **Speakers** tab first).
6.  **Time**: Set the **Start Time** and **End Time**.
7.  **Location**: Specify the room or hall (e.g., "Main Hall, Room 302").
8.  **Description**: Provide a brief summary of what the session covers.
9.  Click **Save Session**.

### Active Session Management

During the event, you can manage live engagement for each ongoing session. Click on a session in the sidebar to open its management panel.

- **Live Polls**: Click **+ Create New Interaction Poll** to launch a question to attendee devices. View results in real-time.
- **Attendee Q&A**: Enable this to allow the audience to submit questions via the app.
- **End Session**: Click the red button to **close the current poll** and finalize the interaction results.

## Speakers

Manage the profiles of the experts and hosts presenting at your event.

### Creating a Speaker
1.  Navigate to the **Speakers** tab.
2.  **Speaker Photo**: Upload a high-quality headshot.
3.  **Details**: Enter **Full Name**, **Role** (e.g., CEO), and **Company**.
4.  **Bio**: Write a short biography introducing the speaker.
5.  **Socials**: Add links to their **LinkedIn** and **Twitter** profiles.
6.  **Link to App User**:
    > [!TIP]
    > **Enable Q&A Access**: Enter the email address the speaker used to sign up for the Yenhyia app. This links their speaker profile to their user account, allowing them to receive Q&A notifications and answer questions directly from their phone.
7.  Click **Create Speaker**.
