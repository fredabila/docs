<Note>
**Access Path**: **Event Dashboard** > **Insights**
</Note>

The **Insights** tab provides deeper visibility into your event's success. It is divided into three sub-menus: **Overview**, **Analytics**, and **Activity Log**.


## Overview

The default view gives you a health check of your event logistics.

- **Total Capacity**: Number of signups and imports relative to your max capacity.
- **Actual Check-ins**: Real-time count of verified attendees on-site.
- **Platform Users**: Number of attendees who are registered users on the Yenhyia app, increasing their networking capabilities.
- **Imported List**: Usage of CSV or manual entry imports.

### Key Actions
From the Overview screen, you can also:
- **Get QR Code**: For staff to scan attendee tickets.
- **View Guestlist**: To manage individual attendees.
- **Open Full Analytics**: To jump directly to the Analytics tab.

## Analytics

This section focuses on engagement and demographics.

### Engagement Metrics
- **Engagement Score**: A percentage indicating how active your attendees are (profile completion, networking).
- **Total Connections**: The total number of connections made between attendees at your event.
- **Live Activity**: A real-time feed of networking actions.

### Demographics & Behavior
- **Active Time of Day**: Heatmap showing when your event page or check-ins are busiest.
- **Top Connectors**: Attendees who are networking the most.
- **Top Roles & Companies**: Breakdown of attendee professional backgrounds.
- **Top Interests**: Aggregated data on what your audience cares about (e.g., Tech & AI, SaaS).

### Ticket Sales
- **Ticket Sales Ledger**: A real-time transaction log of ticket purchases.

## Activity Log

The Activity Log provides an audit trail of administrative actions taken on the event. This is useful for tracking changes made by your team, such as:
- Removing attendees.
- Updating event details.
- Changing ticket tiers.
