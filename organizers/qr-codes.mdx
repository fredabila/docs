The **QR Code** tool is your central station for event promotion and attendee access. It provides everything you need to guide users to your event page and track their engagement through real-time scans.


## Event QR & Brand Kit

This tool generates a dynamic QR code linked specifically to your event.

### Key Features
- **Dynamic QR Code**: A unique code that attendees can scan to check in or join your event.
- **Scan Analytics**: View **Total Scans** in real-time to gauge the success of your promotional efforts.
- **Customization**: Upload an optional **Brand Logo** to be displayed in the center of your QR code for a professional look.
- **Shareable Link**: Quickly copy the direct event URL to share on social platforms.

### Actions
- **Regenerate**: Refresh your QR code if needed.
- **Copy Link**: Grab the shareable link for digital promotion.

## Social & Print Templates

Yenhyia automatically prepares professional assets for you, saving you design time. You can download ready-to-use templates in various formats:

- **Square (Instagram Post)**: 1080×1080
- **Story (TikTok / Reels)**: 1080×1920
- **Poster (Print A4)**: 2480×3508
- **Flyer (Handout A5)**: 1748×2480

## Raw Assets

If you need the source files for custom designs, you can download:
- **Logo SVG**: Vector format for high-quality printing.
- **QR Code PNG**: High-resolution image format.

## Universal QR Scanner

Below the brand kit, you'll find the **Universal QR Scanner**. This tool is used by event staff to:
- Scan attendee tickets for verification.
- Process event join codes.
- Manage real-time check-ins on-site.
